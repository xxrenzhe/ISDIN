Fusion Water MAGIC is ISDIN's broad-spectrum SPF 40 daily facial sunscreen for the U.S. market. The brand describes it as ultralight, fast absorbing, hydrating and free of visible white cast. This is a formula-and-claims guide, not a wear test.

## BES3 verdict

The product may suit someone who wants a fluid, non-mineral daily sunscreen and values a clear finish. It is less obviously suited to swimming or heavy sweating: ISDIN distinguishes the Eryfotona range as its water-resistant option. Eye comfort and pilling claims are encouraging but remain individual in real use.

## What the label tells us

The most important facts are broad-spectrum SPF 40 and the current Drug Facts active-filter panel. Because filter combinations can change by market and reformulation, verify that panel on the product you buy rather than relying on an older online list.

ISDIN highlights Mediterranean algae extract, hyaluronic acid and vitamin E as supporting ingredients. These do not determine the SPF. They are present for antioxidant and hydration positioning around the tested sunscreen formula.

## The brand claims, in context

ISDIN claims twelve-hour hydration, a non-greasy finish, no white cast and protection language extending to blue light, pollution, infrared and heat-related free radicals. The official page cites company testing for some outcomes. BES3 treats these as finished-product brand claims, not a reason to relax ordinary ultraviolet-protection habits.

Broad-spectrum sunscreen, sufficient application and reapplication during sustained exposure remain the core. An antioxidant cannot make an incomplete sunscreen film complete.

## Routine placement

Apply as the final skincare step before makeup. A fluid sunscreen may replace moisturizer for some normal-to-oily users; dry skin may still need a light moisturizer underneath. Shake and apply according to the current label.

If the intended amount pills, simplify the serums below it and try two thin passes. If the eye area stings, use a separate compatible sunscreen there rather than applying less everywhere.

## Compare before choosing

Choose Fusion Water MAGIC over an Eryfotona product when a clear, lightweight daily finish is the priority. Choose a labeled water-resistant sunscreen for sport, swimming or substantial sweating, and consider a tinted mineral option when tint or visible-light concerns are important.
