Melaclear Advanced is ISDIN's targeted discoloration serum featuring tranexamic acid, niacinamide, licorice root extract and a proprietary Spot Corrector Complex. The brand claims visible improvement beginning at two weeks and continued results through twelve weeks. Those timelines are brand claims, not a guarantee for every cause of pigmentation.

## BES3 verdict

The serum may suit someone who wants tranexamic acid and niacinamide in one focused step. It is a poor fit for a routine already overloaded with acids, retinoids and several brightening serums. The product cannot replace assessment of a new or changing spot.

## Morning routine

1. Gentle cleanser or water rinse.
2. Melaclear Advanced, following the current amount directions.
3. Moisturizer if needed.
4. Broad-spectrum high-SPF sunscreen.

Sun protection is not optional in a discoloration plan. Consider a tinted formula with iron oxides when visible-light-sensitive pigmentation, such as melasma, is part of the concern.

## Evening routine

Cleanse, apply Melaclear Advanced and finish with moisturizer. Start once daily even if the package permits more frequent use. After two weeks of comfort, follow the label if you want to increase frequency.

Do not introduce a retinoid or strong exfoliant in the same week. Add only one variable at a time, and alternate nights if both products remain important.

## How to judge progress

Use the serum consistently for eight to twelve weeks before making a measured cosmetic assessment. Take photographs under the same lighting and avoid reading temporary post-cleansing brightness as pigment change.

Stop if the product causes ongoing burning, swelling or a rash. Seek professional advice for rapidly changing, asymmetric, bleeding or symptomatic lesions and for pigmentation that does not fit a familiar pattern.

## Brand claim versus routine reality

ISDIN's ingredient story is plausible for a tone-focused product, but results depend on the cause and depth of pigment, sunscreen habits, irritation and adherence. The most credible routine is the one that reduces new pigment signals while keeping the skin barrier comfortable.
